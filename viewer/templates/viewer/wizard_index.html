New Resume Wizard

The wizard process will help you create the resources to build a resume.

Please read the resource descriptions before proceeding with the wizard.

  • Experience Domain - The overarching categories that your experiences fit into
  • Experience - The relevant experiences to your history
  • Duty - A short description of a duty or responsibility that you were responsible for at one of your employments
  • Project - A short description of a primary or side project that you worked on.
  • Education - An education, certification or other learning related credit.
  • Employment - A place you worked at, associated with duties, projects and references.
  • Reference - A person at an employment who can be contacted for a employment reference.
  • Applicant - Your contact information.
  • Resume - Format, styling and reference name for your new Resume.
